About Us

We are a startup looking to revolutionize the mobility industry by making it accessible and comfortable for everyone.


Tripiando is a platform where if you are planning a trip somewhere and you have extra space in your car, you can earn money for it, or if you are looking to go somewhere easily, you can find people going in the same direction.


Our objective is to give our users control over their journey.
